Meet the Team!

Student Government consists of two branches: Political and Activities. The political branch focuses on addressing student concerns and issues; this year we will be focusing on two main initiatives: Wellness and Going Green. The activities branch plans and organizes major student activities throughout the school year, such as Spirit Week, Frolic, and Field Day. All StuGov officers and members work on political initiatives as well as StuGov activities.
